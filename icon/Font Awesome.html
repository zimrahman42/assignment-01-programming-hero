Nice icon selection!

To hand-pick icons for a Kit, you need a Kit with By Icon selection enabled. Create a new one below and start building your collection.

Already have a Kit? You can switch it to By Icon in your Kit's settings, or just keep it as By Style or Full Library - your call!

What's By Icon selection?

Instead of including thousands of Font Awesome icons in your Kit, you hand-pick just the icons you need for your project to get the lightest, speediest performance.
